## Phase 1: SNIFFING ğŸ‘ƒ

**What:** Mutual discovery. Are we right for each other?

**Activities:**
- Initial conversation
- Understanding their situation
- Sensing pack dynamics
- Assessing fit

**Key Questions:**
- What problem are they actually solving?
- Do I have the skills for this?
- Are they ready for this work?
- Do we work well together?
- Can they pay fair rates?

**Red Flags:**
- Unclear problem definition
- Previous consultant failures with no self-reflection
- Expecting miracles
- Price pressure before understanding value
- Disrespect in initial interactions

**Exit Criteria:**
- Clear enough problem statement
- Mutual interest in proceeding
- No deal-breakers identified

---

## Phase 2: BONDING ğŸ¤

**What:** Formalizing the relationship. Defining the work.

**Activities:**
- Discovery sessions (deep sensing)
- Scope definition
- Proposal writing
- Contract negotiation
- Onboarding

**Deliverables:**
- Discovery findings
- Scope document
- Proposal / Statement of Work
- Signed contract
- Access and logistics setup

**Key Principles:**
- Be specific about scope
- Define what's NOT included
- Clarify communication expectations
- Establish success criteria
- Document assumptions

## Phase 3: RUNNING ğŸ’¨

**What:** Doing the work. Delivering value.

**Rhythms:**
| Activity | Frequency | Purpose |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| Check-in | Weekly | Pulse, blockers, priorities |
| Working session | As needed | Active collaboration |
| Status update | Bi-weekly | Formal progress report |
| Retrospective | Monthly | Process improvement |

**Managing the Engagement:**
- Keep communication channels open
- Surface issues early
- Document decisions
- Track scope changes
- Celebrate milestones

**Scope Change Protocol:**
1. Identify the change
2. Assess impact (time, cost, quality)
3. Document the request
4. Discuss with client
5. Amend agreement if proceeding
6. Document the decision

**What:** Ending well. Setting up for success without you.

**Activities:**
- Knowledge transfer
- Documentation handoff
- Final deliverables
- Retrospective
- Relationship maintenance

**Parting Well:**
- No surprisesâ€”discuss ending early

**After Parting:**

- Periodic check-ins

## Engagement Health Checks

### Weekly Pulse

| Question | Green | Yellow | Red |
|----------|-------|--------|-----|
| Are we on track? | Yes | Minor delays | Significant issues |
| Is communication flowing? | Open, regular | Some gaps | Breakdown |
| Are they engaged? | Responsive, involved | Distracted | Absent |
| Am I energized? | Yes | Tired but okay | Dreading it |

### Monthly Review

- Progress against objectives
- Scope vs. actual
- Relationship health
- Lessons learned
- Adjustments needed

### Pricing Tips

- Quote confidently (you're worth it)
- Include buffer for unknowns (15-25%)
- Define payment terms clearly
- Invoice promptly
- Follow up on late payments professionally
