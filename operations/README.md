## The FIDO Business Model

**Fractional Information & Digital Officer**

Three delivery modes:

| Mode | Description | Best For | Investment |
|------|-------------|----------|------------|
| **Retainer** | Ongoing hours, flexible allocation | Continuous relationships | Monthly fee |
| **Scoped Project** | Defined deliverables, fixed timeline | Specific outcomes | Project fee |
| **Embedded** | Part-time integration with team | Deep partnership | Day/week rate |

Most engagements blend modes. A retainer might include occasional scoped projects. Embedded work might have retainer support afterward.

## Consultancy Health Metrics

### Financial

| Metric | Healthy Range | Warning Sign |
|--------|---------------|--------------|
| Utilization | 60-80% | > 90% (burnout) or < 50% (pipeline issue) |
| Days Sales Outstanding | < 45 days | > 60 days (cash flow risk) |
| Client concentration | < 40% from one client | > 50% (dependency risk) |
| Revenue per hour | Increasing | Flat or decreasing |

### Operational

| Metric | Healthy Sign | Warning Sign |
|--------|--------------|--------------|
| Repeat business | > 50% from existing clients | All new clients (relationship issue) |
| Referrals | Regular inbound | Zero referrals (quality issue) |
| Scope creep | Rare, managed | Constant (boundary issue) |
| Project overruns | < 10% of projects | > 30% (estimation issue) |

### Personal

| Metric | Healthy Sign | Warning Sign |
|--------|--------------|--------------|
| Energy | Sustainable | Chronic fatigue |
| Learning | Regular growth | Stagnation |
| Enjoyment | Most days good | Dread |
| Boundaries | Protected personal time | Work bleeds everywhere |

## Pricing Philosophy

### Value-Based Thinking

Don't price on time alone. Price on value delivered.

| Engagement Type | Value to Client | Pricing Approach |
|-----------------|-----------------|------------------|
| Fire fighting | Urgent problem solved | Premium rates |
| Optimization | Efficiency gains | Share in savings |
| Strategy | Better decisions | Fixed fee for deliverable |
| Capacity | Extra hands | Time-based |

### Rate Setting

| Factor | Increases Rate | Decreases Rate |
|--------|----------------|----------------|
| Expertise | Deep, rare skills | Common skills |
| Urgency | Fire drill | Long timeline |
| Scope | Well-defined | Ambiguous |
| Relationship | New client | Repeat client |
| Geography | High-cost area | Lower-cost area |

## Boundaries

Healthy boundaries = sustainable practice.

### Good Boundaries

- "I don't work weekends except for true emergencies"
- "I need 48 hours notice for meetings"
- "That's outside our agreed scope; let's discuss"
- "I'm not available that week"

### Boundary Violations to Watch

- Clients expecting instant responses
- Scope creep without compensation
- Personal time erosion
- Taking on projects you shouldn't
- Working for free to "prove yourself"
