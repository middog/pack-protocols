## Philosophy

> **Advisory with presence, not ego.**

The best consultants are like good working dogs: attentive without being anxious, helpful without being performative, present without needing to be the center of attention.

Read more: [Philosophy](docs/PHILOSOPHY.md)

Pack Protocols is built on these operating principles:

- **Inclusion** â€” Every voice matters
- **Generosity** â€” Help without expectation of return
- **Capacity Building** â€” Build independence, not dependency
- **Authenticity** â€” Bring your real self to the work
- **Collaboration** â€” Together over solo
- **Stewardship** â€” Care for the whole system
- **Clean Handoffs** â€” Good transitions, good documentation
- **Participation** â€” No spectators; everyone contributes
- **Presence** â€” Be here now
