## Ecosystems & Community

### Kauffman Foundation
Research on entrepreneurship and ecosystem building. The Growth Path framework draws from Kauffman's work on scaling initiatives from experiment to institution.
- [Entrepreneurial Ecosystem Playbook](https://www.kauffman.org/ecosystem-playbook-draft-3/)

## Security

### NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) 2.0
Foundation for Shepherd/vCIO work. Organizes security into GOVERN, IDENTIFY, PROTECT, DETECT, RESPOND, RECOVER.
- [nist.gov/cyberframework](https://www.nist.gov/cyberframework)

### NIST SP 800-171 Rev 3
Security requirements for CUI. Relevant for orgs handling federal data or seeking a rigorous compliance baseline.
- [csrc.nist.gov/publications/detail/sp/800-171/rev-3/final](https://csrc.nist.gov/publications/detail/sp/800-171/rev-3/final)

### CIS Controls
Prioritized cyber defense actions. More prescriptive than NIST CSF — useful when you need specific implementation guidance.
- [cisecurity.org/controls](https://www.cisecurity.org/controls)

## Delivery

### DORA Research
Definitive research on DevOps practices and organizational performance. Key metrics: Deployment Frequency, Lead Time for Changes, Change Failure Rate, Time to Restore.
- [dora.dev](https://dora.dev/)

### Accelerate
Nicole Forsgren, Jez Humble, Gene Kim. The research base behind DevOps. Essential reading for Guide/vCTO work.

## Change Management

### Kotter's 8-Step Model
Create urgency → Form coalition → Shape vision → Communicate → Remove obstacles → Quick wins → Build on change → Anchor in culture.
- [kotterinc.com](https://www.kotterinc.com/methodology/8-steps/)

### ADKAR (Prosci)
Individual change model: Awareness → Desire → Knowledge → Ability → Reinforcement. Useful for tracking where adoption is actually breaking down.
- [prosci.com](https://www.prosci.com/methodology/adkar)

### Bridges' Transition Model
Endings → Neutral Zone → New Beginnings. The psychological arc of transition — distinct from the event of change itself.

## Organizational Research

### Google Project Aristotle
Psychological safety is the #1 predictor of team effectiveness. The research behind why Companion work matters.
- [rework.withgoogle.com](https://rework.withgoogle.com/guides/understanding-team-effectiveness)

### Diffusion of Innovations (Rogers)
Technology adoption lifecycle: Innovators → Early Adopters → Early Majority → Late Majority → Laggards. Informs Companion/adoption work.
