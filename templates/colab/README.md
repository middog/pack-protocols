## The "Yes... And!" Principle

SDCAP and CoLab exist to **enable**, not to **own**.

If your proposal doesn't fit their current capacity, expect:
- "Yes, this is great, AND here's how you could run it yourself"
- "Yes, we want to support this, AND here's what we can offer"

Never just "no." Always "yes, and here's the path."

## What CoLab Is NOT

- Property management

## What SDCAP Is NOT

- Owner of initiatives
- Authority for what can/cannot be done
