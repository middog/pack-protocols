## Opening (5 min)

> "I'm here to understand, not to fix. The goal is to see what you see."

Set expectations:
- This is diagnostic, not prescriptive
- No judgment on current state
- Everything shared stays in context

---

## Pack Sensing Questions

### The Actual Flow

> "Walk me through how [core activity] actually gets doneâ€”not the official process, the real one."

Listen for:
- Workarounds and informal channels
- Who actually does what
- Where things get stuck

> "Who would you go to if you needed [X] quickly?"

Listen for:
- Informal authority
- Connectors and carriers
- Gaps between org chart and reality

### The Unspoken

> "What's the thing everyone knows but nobody says out loud?"

Listen for:
- Taboo topics
- Historical wounds
- Elephants in the room

> "What's working that we should absolutely protect?"

Listen for:
- Hidden strengths
- Things that would break if "fixed"
- What they're proud of

### The Friction

> "Where do things feel harder than they should?"

Listen for:
- Recurring pain points
- Normalized dysfunction
- Energy drains

> "What have you tried before? What happened?"

Listen for:
- Past attempts and failures
- Pattern recognition
- Trust wounds

### The Energy

> "When do people light up here? When do they shut down?"

Listen for:
- Motivation sources
- Burnout indicators
- What actually matters

> "If you could change one thing tomorrow, what would it be?"

Listen for:
- Quick wins
- Core frustrations
- What they actually want

---

## Fire Triangle Probe

### Fuel (Resources)

- "What resources do you have that aren't being fully used?"
- "What do you need that you don't have?"
- "Who controls access to [key resource]?"

### Oxygen (Enablement)

- "What blocks flow right now?"
- "How does information move here?"
- "What process is missing or broken?"

### Heat (Energy)

- "Who cares most about this?"
- "Where does momentum come from?"
- "What would make people actually show up for this?"
