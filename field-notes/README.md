# Field Notes

**The Fire Triangle in Practice**

Where the frameworks meet real organizational situations. Each entry documents an application of the fire triangle diagnostic: which element was thin, what changed, what happened.

Anonymized. Specific. No consulting-speak.

---

## Format

Each field note follows this structure:

```
## Situation
What kind of organization. What they were trying to do. What wasn't working.

## The Diagnostic
Which element of the fire triangle was thin, and how we found out.
Fuel (resources/infrastructure) · Oxygen (process/flow) · Heat (people/participation)

## The Work
What changed. What we did. What the organization did.

## What Happened
The outcome. Honest — including what didn't work.

## What It Taught Us
One thing the situation added to how we use this framework.
```
